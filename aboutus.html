The Evolution of Discovery

Tracing the milestones of map history.

Age of Discovery

15th - 17th Century

Early explorations sparked a revolution in mapmaking. Maps from this era reflect humanity's expanding view of the world, charting newly discovered continents and establishing global trade routes.

Golden Age of Cartography

16th - 17th Century

Dutch mapmakers elevated maps to art forms, incorporating ornate illustrations, mythological sea monsters, and incredibly detailed compass roses that defined the era's aesthetic.

The Scientific Era

18th - 19th Century

Mapmaking shifted towards scientific precision and rigorous surveying. These maps offer a fascinating look at the meticulous development of modern geography and national boundaries.
